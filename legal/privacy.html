Privacy Policy
Effective date: January 1, 2026

1. Overview

Boosted Movers ("we", "us") operates the Analytics Dashboard for internal business use. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

2. Data We Collect

Through your use of the Dashboard and connected integrations, we may collect:

  • Social media analytics — public metrics from YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram (views, likes, comments, follower counts)
  • OAuth tokens — access tokens issued by TikTok when you connect your account; stored securely in Supabase and never shared externally
  • Usage preferences — theme mode (light/dark) stored locally in your browser via localStorage

3. How We Use Your Data

Data collected is used solely to power the Analytics Dashboard for Boosted Movers internal reporting. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

Analytics data and OAuth tokens are stored in Supabase, a secure cloud database. We apply row-level security and access controls. Preference data (e.g., theme) is stored only in your browser and never transmitted to our servers.

5. Third-Party Platforms

The Dashboard integrates with YouTube (Google), TikTok, and Instagram APIs. Your use of these integrations is governed by the respective platform privacy policies:

  • Google / YouTube: policies.google.com/privacy
  • TikTok: tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy

6. Data Retention

TikTok OAuth tokens are automatically invalidated after 24 hours and must be refreshed. Cached analytics data is overwritten each time a fresh fetch succeeds. We do not retain historical personal data beyond what is necessary for dashboard operation.

7. Your Rights

As an authorized Dashboard user, you may request deletion of any stored tokens or cached data at any time by contacting us. You can also disconnect TikTok directly from the Dashboard.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via the Dashboard or email.

9. Contact

Privacy questions or requests? Email us at anthony@boostedmovers.com.
